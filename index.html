About Characters Memory Test

Characters Memory Test is an innovative cognitive training game designed to enhance your memory skills while exposing you to different writing systems from around the world. Whether you're a language enthusiast or simply looking to improve your memory, this game offers an engaging and challenging experience.

How to Play

1. Choose Your Language

Select from 9 different languages: Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, French, Spanish, German, Russian, and Arabic.

2. Memorize Characters

Watch carefully as characters appear on screen. The sequence gets longer as you progress through levels.

3. Reproduce the Sequence

Type the characters in the exact order you saw them. Accuracy is key!

Game Rules

20 Challenging Levels

Progress through 20 levels of increasing difficulty. Each level adds more characters to remember.

Time Management

First 4 levels give you 5 seconds. Each subsequent level adds 1 second for memorization.

Perfect Accuracy

Enter the exact sequence to advance. One mistake, and you'll need to retry the level.
